About me

I am currently a visiting PhD student (under the supervision of Prof. François Gay-Balmaz) at Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in Singapore. I received my B.Sc. in Mathematics from Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT) in 2021 (Supervisor: Prof. Donghua Shi). Now I am also a PhD student (Supervisor: Prof. Jinzhi Wang) majored in Mechanics and Control at Peking University (PKU).

Please feel free to drop me an email for discussions about any interestnig research topics on Geometry, Mechanics, and Control!

Research Interest

My research interest lies at geometric modeling and simulation of dynamical systems, which includes the following topics:

  • Geometric Mechanics (Stochastic & Nonholonomic), Geometric Numerical Integration (Variational Integrators)

  • Geometric Estimation and Filtering (Robot Systems & Flexible Systems)

  • Geometry of Gaussian Distributions (Controlling Uncertainties)

  • Computationally Efficient Physics-Informed Learning

Selected Research

 

Variational unscented Kalman filter on matrix Lie groups
Automatica, 172: 111995, 2025 (Full Paper).
Tianzhi Li and Jinzhi Wang
[Paper PDF]

We propose a family of computationally efficient unscented Kalman filters (UKF-Vs) for mechanical systems on matrix Lie groups.

 

Reduced dynamics and geometric optimal control of nonequilibrium thermodynamics: Gaussian case
Automatica, 164: 111626, 2024 (Full Paper).
Tianzhi Li, Rui Fu, and Jinzhi Wang
[Paper PDF]

We study the geometric structures of n-DOF Gaussian distributions, and we propose a geometric optimal control algorithm for minimum-energy optimal control problem of Gaussian distributions.

 

Physics-Informed Gaussian Process Learning on Lie Groups
Journal of Guidance, Control, and Dynamics (JGCD), accepted, 2025.
Tianzhi Li and Jinzhi Wang

We introduce a physics-informed Gaussian process learning method for mechanical systems on Lie groups and on a special class of homogeneous manifolds based on discrete mechanics theory.
